Restaurants

Middle Village has so much to offer in terms of food! Located in Queens, we are home to dozens of restaurants representing cultures around the world. Here are two of my recommendations:

This is Las Tias Cafe Bakery, located on Metropolitan Avenue. Click here for directions: Google Maps

They serve a variety of Colombian style dishes and bread. My favorite drink is the cholado, which is kind of like a fruity slushy. Here is a feature article I wrote on the place: Article

Also located on Metropolitan Ave is Ralph's Italian Ices, an amazing icecream chain store that started in Long Island. Check out their website here: Ralph's

They have over 30 flavors of ices to choose from. One of my personal favorites is pineapple.
