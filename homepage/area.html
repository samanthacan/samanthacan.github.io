This is our beloved Juniper Valley Park, not far from the very center of the neighborhood. Juniper is where everyone goes to exercise, play sports, or talk under the shade of a tree. It has two playgrounds, basketball courts, a track field, and a large hill that slopes down into a small baseball field. I personally spent a lot of time here growing up as my elementary/middle school was right across from the park.
