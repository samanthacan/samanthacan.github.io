More Surrounding the Town!

As much as I love Middle Village, there are honorary places nearby I couldn't go without mentioning:

Previous Next

Eddie's Sweet Shop is hands down the best icecream place in Queens. It's one of the oldest icecream parlors in NYC (opened in 1925) and has a very nostalgic feel when you enter. Click here for directions: Eddie's

There are 18 flavors of icecream available to order, all made from scratch. The sundaes are the best!

Previous Next

Everyone likes to shop, right? Fortunately, Middle Village is of walking distance to these three malls: Queens Center, The Shops at Atlas, and Rego Center.

One of my favorite things do to pre-covid was walk with my friends to Atlas, watch a movie at Regal Cinemas there, then finish the day with Coldstone icecream. I really recommend it.
